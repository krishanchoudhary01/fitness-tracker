Nutrition Tips

Nutrition is the foundation of any fitness journey. Here are a few quick tips:

  • Eat a balanced mix of carbs, protein, and fats.
  • Stay hydrated — drink at least 2–3 liters of water per day.
  • Include fruits and vegetables in every meal.
  • Track your calories and protein intake.
  • Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks.
